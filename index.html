Bunn's Village Advantages:

  • No "Triple Net" Fees - No hidden fees, period, and no unexpected rate fluctuations from month to month.
  • Private Water & Sewer Systems - No additional utility costs for water and sewer services-- it's already included in your monthly rate.
  • Flexibility - Need room to expand? No problem. Bunn's Village offers a variety of building sizes, including a number of half-units (with more on the way).
  • Electricity - All units feature 240V service, and over 90% are pre-wired for three-phase power. Whatever your power needs, you're covered. Service provided by McMinnville Water and Light.
  • Heating and Air Conditioning - Both come standard on all units. All bathrooms feature hot water heaters.
  • Access and Convenience - Plenty of off-street parking. Lots of space makes unloading easy-- even for semi trucks. Plus, all buildings feature overhead doors, each a minimum of 10'x10'.
  • Location - Just outside of McMinnville on US Highway 99 with great visibility and access, and Neighborhood Commercial zoning means flexibility.
  • Appearance - We are constantly updating and upgrading our buildings. A few of our buildings are essentially brand-new, featuring new exterior metal, fresh paint, and concrete floors.

We require a one year minimum lease and proof of insurance. See our rental application for more details.
